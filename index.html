Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what our customers ask most.

Do you supply Dalmia and Ultratech cement? ▾
Yes! We are authorized dealers for Dalmia, Ultratech, Ramco, Zuari and Chettinad cement. All grades — OPC 43, OPC 53 and PPC — are available in bulk. Call us for current bag prices and availability.
Do you deliver materials directly to the construction site? ▾
Absolutely. We provide site delivery across Chennai and surrounding areas. Our vehicles handle large loads including cement bags, TMT bars, sand and more. Same-day dispatch is available for in-stock items — just call us in advance.
What brands do you supply? ▾
We are authorized dealers for 50+ top brands including Ultratech, Dalmia, Ramco, Asian Paints, Finolex, Ashirvad, Parryware, Sintex, Havells, Polycab, Legrand, Dr Fixit, and many more. Visit our Brands page for the full list.
How do I get the current price for materials? ▾
Prices vary with market conditions. For the most accurate and up-to-date pricing, call us on 98409 28767 or WhatsApp 98409 28767. You can also use our "Request Quote" form and we'll respond within minutes.
Do you offer bulk pricing for contractors? ▾
Yes! We have a dedicated contractor support program with special pricing, priority stock allocation and credit facility for regular buyers. Contact us to discuss your project requirements and get a customized quote.
Where is Kuber Agencies located? ▾
We are based in Madambakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu — easily accessible from Tambaram, Chromepet, Medavakkam and surrounding areas. Our store is open Monday–Saturday 8AM to 8PM and Sunday 9AM to 2PM.

Our Story

About Kuber Agencies

Kuber Agencies is a premium construction materials supplier based in Madambakkam, Chennai. Founded in 2000, with over 25 years of experience, we have grown into one of South Chennai's most trusted hardware and building materials dealers, serving residential builders, commercial contractors, and individual homeowners alike.

We are authorized dealers for more than 50 top brands including Ultratech, Dalmia, Asian Paints, Finolex, Westar, and many more. Our wide product range — from cement and steel to tiles, paints, pipes and electrical items — means our customers can source everything they need from a single reliable partner.

Our strength lies in our relationships — with our brand partners, our delivery teams, and most importantly, our customers. We offer bulk pricing, site delivery across Chennai, and dedicated support for contractors and project managers.
