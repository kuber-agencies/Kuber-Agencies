Our Story

About Kuber Agencies

Kuber Agencies is a premium construction materials supplier based in Madambakkam, Chennai. Founded in 2000, with over 25 years of experience, we have grown into one of South Chennai's most trusted hardware and building materials dealers, serving residential builders, commercial contractors, and individual homeowners alike.

We are authorized dealers for more than 50 top brands including Ultratech, Dalmia, Asian Paints, Finolex, Westar, and many more. Our wide product range — from cement and steel to tiles, paints, pipes and electrical items — means our customers can source everything they need from a single reliable partner.

Our strength lies in our relationships — with our brand partners, our delivery teams, and most importantly, our customers. We offer bulk pricing, site delivery across Chennai, and dedicated support for contractors and project managers.
